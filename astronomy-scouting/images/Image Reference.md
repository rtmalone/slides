If any downloaded images are too small for your slides:

- **Ed White spacewalk:** Search NASA ID "GPN-2006-000025" or visit nasa.gov/gallery/ed-white/
- **Neil Armstrong portrait:** Search NASA ID "S69-31741" at images.nasa.gov
- **Earthrise:** Full resolution at images.nasa.gov/details-as08-14-2383.html
- **InOMN graphics:** Additional banners/social media at science.nasa.gov/moon/observe-the-moon-night/
